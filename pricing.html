    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q
    How to buy a subscription?
    A
    1. Log IN to your TMetric account. If you don't have one, sign up now.
    2. On the main menu, click Workspace and select Subscription.
    3. On the subscription page, click Upgrade Now.
    4. Enter your team size, select a subscription plan, and click Proceed To Checkout.
    5. On the shopping cart page, enter your Billing Information and select Payment Options.
    6. Click Continue to place an order.
    Q
    Do you have volume discounts?
    A

      Yes, we provide volume discounts for big teams according to the following scheme:

    1. 10% for workspaces with 40 or more users;
    2. 20% for workspaces with 100 or more users;.
    3. 30% for workspaces with 200 or more users;.
    4. 40% for workspaces with 500 or more users.

      5. The price will be automatically discounted when you proceed to the payment gateway.
